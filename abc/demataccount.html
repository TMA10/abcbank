Demat Account

Features of Demat Account:
  • No account opening charges.
  • Waiver on the 1st year AMC.
  • Transaction linked with Annual Maintenance Charges (AMC), from the 2nd year onwards.
  • Preferential pricing for the Imperia, Preferred and Classic customers.
  • No stamp duty (A tax levied on documents, stamp duty applies to documents like cheques, receipts, licenses and transaction records) on transfer of securities held in Demat form.
  • View Holding and Transaction details on NetBanking.
